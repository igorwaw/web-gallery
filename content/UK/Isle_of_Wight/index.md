description: I lived on the Isle of Wight in 2022 and 2023. It's the most unusual part of the UK. An important tourist destination for most of the 19th and 20th century, now it's a shadow of its former glory, but still attracts visitors. Also the place where hovercraft and space rockets were built, and where some unscathed nature still remains.
